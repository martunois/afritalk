Gĩkũyũ — The Living Language

40 weeks. 5 stages. Built from the inside — by a daughter learning her mother's tongue so she could finally answer the butcher who greeted her with "Ũhoro..." without stammering. Language as identity. Learning as homecoming.

Why This Course Exists

Gĩkũyũ is one of Kenya's most widely spoken languages — and one of its most at-risk among younger generations. Many Kikuyu people in their 20s and 30s, especially in cities and diaspora, understand fragments of the language but cannot speak it confidently. They grew up hearing it but weren't taught in it.

This course was built for them. And for anyone who wants to connect with Gĩkũyũ culture, oral wisdom, and the people who carry it.

"Gũtirĩ mũndũ ũrĩa ũngĩmenyeka ũtarĩ na rurimi rwe."
No one can truly be known without their language.
